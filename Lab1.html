About Me

My name is Maston Leit. I live in Lone Tree, CO with my wife. I am originally from Honolulu, Hawaii but left when I was 8 and grew up on the east coast in Jacksonville, NC. I ended up there due to my Father in Law being in the Marine Corp. I grew up in a military family and had a lot of relatives and close friends joining a variety of the military branches. I decided once I graduated from high school that I was going to join as well and so I ended up in the US Army as an engineer in a flight company and got to do some cool things and travel Europe. I decided it was time to end my journey in the military and had always wanted to go back to school and get a degree. So, in August 2018 i got out of the service and quickly enrolled into school. I ended up finding the Cybersecurity Program here in 2019 and have worked to get my foot into the Defense Aerospace company Lockheed Martin. I worked there for about a year as a Systems Engineer testing their older defense missile satellite program known as SBIRS(space-based infrared system). Recently I was able to get my Security+ and moved on to a contracting company as a Cybersecurity Engineer working under the SBIRS Program again at Buckley Space Force(I work in the restricted area where those big golf balls are). I have enjoyed my time in the military and continue to do it in school and my current job.
